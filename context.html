By now it’s valuable to see this logo in context. Here is a small mockup that uses the skintones of the logo as a base for all colors and provides editing functionality.

The idea is that the logo will do its own thing until the user decides to interact with it. It will then switch into an editing mode where the segments and text can be tweaked. By having this divide, the interactivity of the logo does not distract too much from the content, but it still enriches the website in a nice way that connects to the Hack the Body identity.
